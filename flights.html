Flight information and rules

Flights are available everyday of the week Sunday - Saturday. While Covid-19 has mostly died down; with new sicknesses such as bird flu emerging, we do require you wear a face mask throughout the flight, except while eating or drinking. We don't allow pets on board, so if you're traveling with an animal, it will be kept in a special creature base on the bottom part of the plane; If you are bringing a pet, please let us know at least 7 days ahead of time. We hope you enjoy your flight!.
