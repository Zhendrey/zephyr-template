Top 10 reasons to visit

Tokyo, Japan

  1. 1. Shibuya crossing

    One of the busiest and most iconic intersections in the world, perfect for people watching and that classic Tokyo photo.

  2. 2.Tokyo Sky-tree

    The tallest structure in Japan offers panoramic views of the city, plus a great shopping and dining complex below.

  3. 3.Senso-ji Temple (Asakusa)

    Tokyo's oldest temple, rich in history and culture, with a lively market street (Nakamise-dori) full of snacks and souvenirs.

  4. 4.Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden

    A peaceful escape with beautiful seasonal blooms especially stunning during cherry blossom season.

  5. 5. Meiji Shrine

    Nestled in a forest near Harajuku, this Shinto shrine is tranquil and deeply spiritual a contrast to the busy streets nearby.

  6. 6. Akihabara

    A paradies for anime, manga, and tech lovers. Explore arcades, maid cafes, and rare collectibles.

  7. 7.Tsukiji Outer Market

    A food lover's heaven, try fresh sushi, wagyu skewers, tamago, and more, it's bustling and full flavor.

  8. 8. Teamlab Planets Tokyo

    An immersive digital art museum where you walk through water, mirrors, and interactive light space. Truly mind-blowing.

  9. 9.Odaiba

    A futuristic entertainment island with shopping, a giant Gundam statue, and hot spring theme parks. Bonus: epic views of the Rainbow bridge.

  10. 10. Tokyo Disneyland & DisneySea

    World-famous theme parks with unique rides and attractions. DisneySea, in particular, is exclusive to Japan and absolutely magical.
